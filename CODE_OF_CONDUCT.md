# Code of Conduct / Magatartási Kódex

We, as contributors and maintainers of this project, pledge to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone. We value the participation of individuals regardless of their age, race, ethnicity, nationality, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, religion, or any other protected characteristic.

## Our Standards / Elvárásaink

We expect all participants to adhere to the following standards of behavior:

- Be respectful and considerate towards others.
- Refrain from any form of harassment, discrimination, or offensive language.
- Be open to constructive feedback and ideas.
- Maintain a professional and inclusive atmosphere.
- Exercise empathy and understanding towards others' perspectives and experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior / Elfogadhatatlan magatartás

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Offensive, derogatory, or discriminatory comments or jokes.
- Personal attacks or insults.
- Any form of trolling or disruptive behavior.
- Any other behavior that creates an unwelcoming or hostile environment.

## Consequences / Következmények

Violations of the code of conduct may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from the project community. The severity of the consequences will be determined by the project maintainers.

## Reporting / Jelentés

If you witness or experience any behavior that violates the code of conduct, please report it to the project maintainers immediately. All reports will be kept confidential and will be reviewed and addressed promptly.
